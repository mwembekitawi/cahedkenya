About Us

Get Know About Us!

COMUNITY ACTION FOR HEALTH & DEVELOPMENT: Is Kenyan Community Based youth serving organization for young women and girls rights non-profit organization based in Kenya founded in Homa Bay with a young volunteer to do service above self. We champion, expand, actualize, aspire to support all communities regardless of gender, culture, religion and socio-economic background, have the right to grow up in secure environment without discrimination.

. We create rights awareness, mobilize and raise resources to help support people’s rights and well-being; ensuring every person has the opportunity to reach their development and potential for free and just society. For more than 12 years, CAHED has been committed to protecting and caring for children and those in need, we have gone where others would not
