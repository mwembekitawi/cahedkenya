Secretariat

Our dedicated team at CAHED Secretariat sets the guardrails for radical collaboration and enables progress towards making programs the norm in Homabay County Kenya. Radical collaboration means that Secretariat growth has a “leverage” effect for the beneficiaries and the field activities: as we reinforce our backbone, we invest in tapping the shared expertise of our staff members, our partners and allies, collaborators, coalitions, alliances, Networks and thousands of beneficiaries who are ready and eager to advance our mission. The Secretariat is guided by an overall ethos of remaining as lean as possible - “hiring when it hurts” - while simultaneously prioritizing sustainability so that our teammates can stay in the fight for as long as it takes
